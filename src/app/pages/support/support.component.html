Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a virtual meeting?

Simply click on the meeting link sent to your email. No installation required - it works directly in your browser.

Is my meeting secure?

Yes. We support end-to-end encryption since our front-end uses your system or mobile browser.

How many users can join my meeting?

75 concurrent participants and 1000+ viewers in each meeting room.

What browsers are supported?

All modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge are fully supported.

How do I test my audio and video before a meeting?

Use our interactive test tool to check your camera, microphone, and speakers. Test your equipment now.
